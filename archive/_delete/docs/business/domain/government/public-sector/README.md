### Why GTCX?

Public sector stakeholders are at the heart of building transparent, compliant gold markets:

- **Policy Alignment**\
  Leverage GTCX’s open-source protocols to meet OECD, LBMA and regional compliance standards.
- **Operational Efficiency**\
  Streamline licensing, traceability, and export approvals using modular playbooks and API blueprints.
- **Economic Impact**\
  Empower local miners, boost tax revenues, and attract institutional buyers by proving legitimacy with cryptographic credentials.

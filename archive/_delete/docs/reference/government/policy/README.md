# Policy

This section curates policy‑facing briefs and concept papers that support public‑sector adoption of GTCX infrastructure.

## Executive Summary

Policy adoption hinges on three outcomes:

- **Regulatory legitimacy**: evidence‑based compliance that improves oversight.
- **Economic impact**: formalization that increases revenue capture and market access.
- **Interoperability**: standards that work across agencies and borders.

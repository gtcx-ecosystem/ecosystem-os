# Call Scripts

## 15-Minute Discovery Call

1. Context: "GTCX is building trust infrastructure for African commodity markets."
2. Their role: "How does your team touch {trade/finance/compliance/commodities}?"
3. Pain: "Where does verification, provenance, compliance, or buyer confidence slow things down?"
4. Current workflow: "How do you handle this today?"
5. Fit: "The possible overlap is {pilot/partnership/revenue/capital}."
6. Next step: "Would it be useful to send {brief/proposal/demo agenda}?"

## Investor Call

1. Thesis: African commodity markets lose value because legitimate producers cannot prove trust at market speed.
2. Product: GTCX creates reusable trust infrastructure across identity, location, compliance, custody, and transaction readiness.
3. Wedge: {country_or_corridor} + {commodity_or_partner}.
4. Business model: pilots, verification services, transaction fees, advisory, data/infrastructure products.
5. Ask: {amount_or_intro_or_design_partner}.
6. Proof needed: "The next milestone is {milestone}."
7. Close: "What would you need to see to take the next step?"

## DFI / Sovereign / Trade Body Call

1. Public-interest frame: formalization, market access, compliance evidence, trade facilitation.
2. Country/corridor: {country_or_corridor}.
3. Stakeholders: producers, buyers, regulators, banks, DFIs.
4. Pilot shape: small, measurable, evidence-producing.
5. Governance: data sovereignty, human approval, no regulatory overclaiming.
6. Next step: technical scoping or stakeholder-mapping session.

## Buyer / Refiner / Trader Call

1. Buyer problem: trust, provenance, compliance, and supplier risk.
2. Current workflow: what documents/evidence they require now.
3. Gap: what they still cannot verify.
4. GTCX fit: identity, GeoTag, GCI/compliance evidence, custody, transaction readiness.
5. Commercial path: pilot, API/integration, verification service, sourcing partnership.
6. Next step: compare one real workflow or sample transaction.

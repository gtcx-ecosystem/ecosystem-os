# WhatsApp Templates

Use WhatsApp for warm relationships, event follow-up, field partners, and quick
coordination. Do not use it for legal, financial, or investment commitments.

## Warm Follow-Up

```text
Hi {first_name}, good speaking earlier. Sharing the quick GTCX angle: we’re building trust infrastructure for African commodity markets — verification, compliance evidence, and market-access workflows. I’ll send the brief by email as well.
```

## Meeting Reminder

```text
Hi {first_name}, confirming our GTCX call at {time}. The main topic is {topic}. I’ll keep it focused: context, fit, and one practical next step.
```

## Event Follow-Up

```text
Hi {first_name}, great meeting at {event}. The useful overlap seems to be {overlap}. Can I send a short GTCX concept note to your email?
```

## Field / Partner Check-In

```text
Hi {first_name}, quick check on {topic}. For GTCX, the useful details are: country, commodity, current workflow, buyer/regulator pain, and who owns the next decision.
```

## Intro Request

```text
Hi {first_name}, do you know the right person at {target_org} for {topic}? A warm intro would help us avoid a cold approach. I can send a 3-line blurb.
```

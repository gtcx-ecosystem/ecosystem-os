# Email Templates

All emails are drafts until human-approved.

## Investor Intro

Subject:

```text
GTCX — African commodity trust infrastructure
```

Body:

```text
Hi {first_name},

I’m reaching out from GTCX. We are building Africa/Global South trust infrastructure for commodity markets: producer identity, field verification, compliance evidence, custody, settlement, and market-access workflows.

The immediate opportunity is {specific_opportunity}: {country_or_corridor}, {commodity_or_market}, and {proof_or_partner_signal}.

Why this may fit {fund_or_org}: {investor_thesis_match}.

Would you be open to a short conversation next week? I can share a concise brief and the current pilot/evidence path.

Best,
{sender}
```

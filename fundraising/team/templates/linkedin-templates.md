# LinkedIn Templates

LinkedIn is manual/human-reviewed. Do not automate scraping or bulk outreach.

## Connection Request

```text
Hi {first_name} — I’m building GTCX from Johannesburg: trust infrastructure for African commodity markets, trade, and compliance. Your work on {specific_topic} looks closely aligned. Would be glad to connect.
```

## Post Reply

```text
This is directly relevant to what we’re seeing in African commodity markets: the gap is not only capital, but trusted verification that buyers, banks, regulators, and producers can all use. Would be interested to compare notes.
```

## After Connection

```text
Thanks for connecting, {first_name}. GTCX is focused on verified commodity producers, responsible sourcing, and African market infrastructure. I noticed your work on {specific_topic}. Is there a good person on your side who thinks about {pilot_or_partner_area}?
```

## Warm Intro DM

```text
Hi {first_name} — quick ask. Do you know someone at {target_org} focused on {topic}? We’re exploring whether GTCX’s verification infrastructure could fit their work in {country_or_corridor}. Happy to send a short forwardable blurb.
```

## Event Follow-Up

```text
Good meeting you around {event}. The overlap I heard was {specific_overlap}. If useful, I can send a concise GTCX brief on {topic} and suggest one concrete next step.
```

# Forensic Africa Opportunity Landscape 2026

This is the live opportunity landscape for GTCX as an American founder based in
Johannesburg building Africa/Global South commodity trust infrastructure. It is
not a US grant list. It is a tracking map for funding, pilots, venture exposure,
technology credits, ecosystem relationships, and events that can help GTCX win
across the continent.

## GTCX Filters

Track an opportunity if it maps to at least one of these:

1. Africa / Global South implementation.
2. Commodity trade, responsible sourcing, digital trade, or market access.
3. African bank, DFI, regulator, ministry, trade body, or buyer relationship.
4. Producer-network, cooperative, women-operator, field/offline, or local-language distribution.
5. Strategic exposure to Africa-focused investors, corporates, DFIs, or government stakeholders.
6. Useful cloud, AI, infrastructure, legal, security, or startup-operating credits.

Do not prioritize generic startup awards, generic US grants, or broad SaaS
accelerators unless they create one of those outcomes.

## Opportunity Classes

| Class | Why It Matters | Route |
| ----- | -------------- | ----- |
| Sovereign/regulator pilots | National trust infrastructure, minerals/trade dashboards, country credibility | Partnership + implementation |
| African bank / DFI pilots | GTX Markets and ComplianceOS design partners; revenue and legitimacy | Paid pilot + strategic partnership |
| Commodity buyers, refiners, traders, vaults | Direct commercial pull for verified supply and provenance | Revenue + integration |
| Producer networks and cooperatives | Verified production data asset and field proof | Pilot + distribution |
| Pan-African trade infrastructure | AfCFTA alignment, corridor credibility, multi-country expansion | Strategic partnership |
| Africa venture / catalytic capital | Funds infrastructure after proof | Equity/catalytic capital |
| Selective Global South challenges | Non-dilutive capital when implementation-aligned | Catalytic capital |
| Cloud / AI / startup credits | Extends runway and compute for agentic infrastructure | Ops savings |

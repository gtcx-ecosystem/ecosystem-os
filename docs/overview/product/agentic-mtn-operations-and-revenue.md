## Revenue Model (Integrated)

### Revenue Streams per User

| Stream                     | Monthly per User | 5,000 Users/Month | 50,000 Users/Month |
| -------------------------- | ---------------- | ----------------- | ------------------ |
| Device lease               | $35              | $175,000          | $1,750,000         |
| Platform fee (2% of sales) | $30 avg          | $150,000          | $1,500,000         |
| Financial services         | $15 avg          | $75,000           | $750,000           |
| Input marketplace (margin) | $10 avg          | $50,000           | $500,000           |
| Data/insights (B2B)        | $5 allocated     | $25,000           | $250,000           |
| Training/certification     | $3 avg           | $15,000           | $150,000           |
| **Total ARPU**             | **$98**          | **$490,000**      | **$4,900,000**     |

### MTN Revenue Share

| Stream             | MTN Share   | 5,000 Users     | 50,000 Users      |
| ------------------ | ----------- | --------------- | ----------------- |
| Device lease       | 30%         | $52,500         | $525,000          |
| MoMo transactions  | 60% of fees | $45,000         | $450,000          |
| Platform fees      | 20%         | $30,000         | $300,000          |
| Financial services | 50%         | $37,500         | $375,000          |
| **Total MTN**      |             | **$165,000/mo** | **$1,650,000/mo** |

**MTN Motivation:** $1.65M/month from 50,000 users = $19.8M/year from a segment they currently can't monetize effectively. Plus strategic ESG positioning and government relationships.

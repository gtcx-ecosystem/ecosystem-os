# Product Marketing

Product, feature, workflow, and launch visual direction.

## Direction

Record Ecosystem OS-specific source prompts, review notes, usage constraints, and approved asset patterns for fleet documentation, GitBook publishing, GTM narratives, onboarding, and architecture storytelling.

## Storage

Generated binaries belong under `platform/assets/generated/media/` and must be indexed in `machine/media/library.manifest.json` before use.

## Review Rules

- Generated assets are illustrative unless explicitly sourced as product screenshots or approved evidence.
- Do not create fake credentials, certificates, seals, badges, QR codes, official forms, or partner proof.
- Do not use generated assets as audit evidence, compliance proof, identity proof, or inspection evidence.

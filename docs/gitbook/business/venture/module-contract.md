# Venture module contract

## Purpose

Make innovation and portfolio strategy **legible, investable, and executable** across the fleet.

Venture turns market insight and product architecture into strategic bets, venture profiles, fundraising narratives, and IP theses that Agency can express and Agile can ship.

## Inputs

- Product architecture and module map (`bridge-os/pm/spec/ecosystem-module-repo-map.json`)
- Fleet repo evidence (audits, pilots, golden transactions)
- Market research and customer doctrine (Ethos, business GitBook)
- Operator transcripts and fundraising source intelligence

## Outputs

- **Portfolio SoR**: product-portfolio, business thesis, packaging table
- **Venture profiles**: per-repo briefs for internal pilot clients
- **Innovation register**: strategic bets with owner repo + evidence
- **Fundraising alignment**: execution backlog traced to pm stories
- **Handoffs**: venture-os product repo for implementation

## Hard ship gates (Venture module)

1. **Commercial value**: portfolio narrative links every operating module to buyer promise
2. **Trust & Safety**: no fundraising claim without evidence path
3. **Ecosystem integration**: fleet profiles exist for build-lane repos
4. **Technical Excellence**: pm/backlog venture slice traces to initiatives.json

## Boundaries

- Venture invents; Agency expresses; Agile delivers; Bridge coordinates.
- venture-os implements the Venture **product** — this module owns **strategy SoR** in ecosystem-os.

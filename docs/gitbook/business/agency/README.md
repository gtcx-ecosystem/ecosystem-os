# Agency — AI-native studio

Agency is the **world-class AI-native creative studio** operating module for GTCX.

It produces brand systems, campaigns, visual media, templates, and communications assets with a governed pipeline: **brief → generate → review → register → publish**.

## What Agency owns

- Brand system + templates (letterhead, business cards, one-pagers, slides)
- Campaign packs (launch narratives, social kits, demo scripts)
- Tooling + stock catalogs (open-source and commercial alternatives; free asset sources)
- Provenance rules (license, attribution, claim→witness linking, approvals)

## Boundaries

- **Venture → Agency**: Venture invents (thesis, IP, strategy). Agency expresses (creative, comms, media, campaigns).
- **Agency → Canon**: Agency creates campaign assets and narratives. Canon publishes official knowledge surfaces.

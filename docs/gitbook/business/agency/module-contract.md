# Agency module contract

## Purpose

Make market expression **repeatable, world-class, and provable**.

Agency turns Venture substance and operating proof into external-facing assets: brand systems, campaigns, demos, decks, one‑pagers, and communications kits.

## Inputs

- Venture substance: theses, strategy, positioning, IP narratives
- Proof sources: witnesses and authoritative references (Canon, repo evidence, published audits)
- Audience context: ICP/persona and desired action (what should the reader do next?)

## Outputs

- **Brand system**: tokens, voice, layout rules, export formats
- **Templates**: one‑pager, slide theme, social templates, email/press templates
- **Campaign packs**: narrative arc, asset list, claim→witness map, channel plan
- **Catalogs**: tools + stock assets + resource sites, with license governance

## Hard ship gates (Agency)

Agency is shippable only when all are true:

1. **Compliance**: licenses/attribution/restrictions complete; no unknown licenses
2. **Trust & Safety**: no public claim without a witness link
3. **Craft**: quality bar met; templates adhere to brand rules
4. **Technical Excellence**: registries validate; checks emit witnesses deterministically

## Boundaries

- Venture invents; Agency expresses.
- Canon publishes official knowledge surfaces; Agency ships campaign assets and narrative packs.

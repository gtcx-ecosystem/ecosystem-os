# Business Thesis

## Category

Ecosystem OS is an AI-native company operating system.

The category sits between enterprise search, agent platforms, workflow automation, governance platforms, knowledge management, PM tools, service operations, and compliance software. The opportunity is to unify these into an operating layer built for human-agent companies from the start.

## Problem

Organizations are adopting agents before they have operating infrastructure for agents.

Common symptoms:

1. Agents have tools but no durable company context.
2. Workflows execute but do not produce replayable evidence.
3. PM, service ops, compliance, HR, docs, and agent tooling live in separate systems.
4. RAG retrieves context but does not know operating truth.
5. Human doctrine, judgment, and leadership context are not machine-actionable.
6. Innovation, marketing, delivery, operations, and assurance are disconnected.

## Customer

Initial customers are AI-native operators and teams with enough complexity to need an operating system:

| Segment | Need |
| ------- | ---- |
| AI-native startups | Run a small team with high agent leverage and strong evidence discipline. |
| Multi-product companies | Coordinate work, services, delivery, and risk across product surfaces. |
| Regulated operators | Prove work, preserve evidence, route approvals, and replay actions. |
| Open-source ecosystems | Coordinate maintainers, contributors, agents, releases, docs, and governance. |
| Venture studios and company builders | Move from thesis to product to market with humans and agents in one loop. |

## Wedge

The first wedge should be an internal operating control plane:

```text
Graph + Bridge + Assurance
```

This gives the system the ability to know company state, coordinate agents and humans, and prove what happened. Agile, Fabric, Ethos, Venture, and Agency expand the product from control plane into complete company OS.

## Commercial expansion

| Stage | Product motion |
| ----- | -------------- |
| Internal reference | Run GTCX with Ecosystem OS; publish proof through Canon OS. |
| Open-source core | Release selected schemas, CLI, MCP servers, and module templates. |
| Team edition | Hosted Surface, Graph, Ledger, Bridge, and Agile for AI-native teams. |
| Enterprise edition | Governance, SSO, audit, policy, private deployments, service integrations. |
| Ecosystem edition | Multi-company graph, partner network, compliance evidence exchange. |

## Strategic moat

The moat is not any one agent, dashboard, or workflow.

The moat is the operating graph plus ledger history plus module contracts plus human doctrine plus accumulated execution evidence.

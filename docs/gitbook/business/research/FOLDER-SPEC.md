# `docs/research/`

## Purpose

Research backing for Ecosystem OS as an AI-native company operating system.

Use this folder for market landscape, technical research, competitor analysis, research questions, and source-backed positioning.

| File | Role |
| ---- | ---- |

## Rules

1. Research supports foundation and business docs; it does not become product doctrine by itself.
2. Prefer primary sources, papers, and official product materials where possible.
3. Separate what competitors claim from what Ecosystem OS should own.
4. Keep source links close to claims.

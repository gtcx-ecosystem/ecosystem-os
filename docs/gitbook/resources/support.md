# Support

If you need help, contact the support team through your enterprise account manager or the primary support channel configured for your deployment.

## Support Channels

| Channel              | Availability   | Notes                                    |
| -------------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Enterprise support   | Business hours | Primary channel for enterprise customers |
| Emergency escalation | 24/7           | SEV1/SEV2 incidents only                 |

## Service Level Targets

| Severity | Initial Response  | Update Frequency |
| -------- | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| SEV1     | 15 minutes        | Every 30 minutes |
| SEV2     | 1 hour            | Every 2 hours    |
| SEV3     | 4 hours           | Daily            |
| SEV4     | Next business day | As needed        |

### II. Background and Regulatory Context

#### Historical Regulatory Challenges

Regulatory bodies worldwide face challenges stemming from outdated compliance systems, fragmented data management, and reliance on manual processes. These shortcomings hinder transparency, increase risks of fraud, and limit real-time oversight capabilities.

#### Contemporary Regulatory Pressures

Today's regulators are tasked with navigating increasingly complex international standards such as OECD due diligence, LBMA responsible sourcing, and stringent ESG requirements. Achieving transparency, real-time compliance, and secure traceability is imperative.

### III. GTCX Vision and Principles

- **Sovereign Data Control:** Ensuring regulators retain full authority over compliance data.
- **Real-Time Traceability:** Instantaneous verification and traceability throughout supply chains.
- **Modular Compliance Framework:** Adaptable modules easily tailored to specific commodities and jurisdictions.
- **Transparency and Auditability:** Secure, auditable records ensuring transparency and regulatory integrity.

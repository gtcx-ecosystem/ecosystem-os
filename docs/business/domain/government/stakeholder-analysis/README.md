# Stakeholder Analysis

Overview: AGX aims to create an integrated African gold marketplace – combining physical gold vaults, tokenized ownership, and a digital compliance layer. In theory, it brings transparency and liquidity to African gold markets; in practice, it must navigate complex realities. African gold supply chains are fragmented, opaque and often informal. Hundreds of tons of gold are smuggled out annually, and many artisanal miners lack formal records. AGX’s business model implicitly assumes miners will register production, that buyers will trust digital gold tokens, and that a blockchain can enforce compliance. These assumptions carry major risks and flaws unless mitigated by rigorous design (see below).

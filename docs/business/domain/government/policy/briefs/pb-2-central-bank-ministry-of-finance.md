# Policy Brief 2: Central Bank and Ministry of Finance

## Purpose

Frame GTCX as infrastructure for financial integrity, settlement assurance, and sovereign oversight of commodity flows.

## Key Outcomes

- Reduced illicit financial flows
- Evidence‑based compliance
- Improved pricing transparency

## Recommended Actions

- Align regulatory reporting with verified data streams
- Pilot settlement proof requirements
- Define interoperability standards with financial systems

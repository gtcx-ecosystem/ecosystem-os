# FEAT-ECO-SIGNAL-MATURITY — governed agentic maturity uplift

Raise Kernel, Graph, Ledger, Surface, Ethos, Venture, and Agency from the inherited repo-level SIGNAL L2 baseline to independently evidenced L5, with L3 and L4 retained as required intermediate proof gates.

## Scope

- Define module-specific SIGNAL-P and SIGNAL-E completion signals.
- Establish a shared trace and evidence envelope across the governed operating loop.
- Enforce trace, policy, approval, eval, rollback, and learning-loop gates.
- Replace inherited module scores with scoped witnesses.
- Require three consecutive passing operating cycles before L4.
- Require five consecutive passing generative operating cycles before L5.
- Require self-improvement, generative design, policy synthesis, cross-module learning, autonomous experimentation, and human override evidence before L5.

## Acceptance

- `pnpm kaleidoscope:signal:remediation:check` passes.
- All seven modules have separate SIGNAL-P/E evidence paths, metrics, release owners, escalation paths, and named implementation partners.
- One replayable end-to-end workflow satisfies the L3 evidence contract.
- No module maturity claim exceeds its lowest dimension or missing evidence set.
- L4 remains blocked until three consecutive scoped release witnesses pass.
- L5 remains blocked until five consecutive scoped operating-cycle witnesses and all six L5 evidence sets pass.
- `pnpm ops:check` and Kaleidoscope release gates fail on remediation contract drift or dishonest maturity claims.

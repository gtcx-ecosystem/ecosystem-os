# Policy Series 3: Training as Trade Infrastructure

## Thesis

Compliance training should be treated as infrastructure, not a discretionary cost.

## Focus

- Workforce capacity as a prerequisite for market access
- Training pipelines tied to verification outcomes
- Public‑private models for scalable delivery

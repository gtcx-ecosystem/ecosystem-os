# Policy Series 2: Open Source, Open Opportunity

## Thesis

Open standards and open infrastructure reduce monopoly risk and broaden access for governments and producers.

## Focus

- Transparency as a trust enabler
- Lower cost of adoption
- Interoperability across jurisdictions

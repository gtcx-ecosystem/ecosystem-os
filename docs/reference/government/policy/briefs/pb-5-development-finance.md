# Policy Brief 5: Development Finance Institutions

## Purpose

Show how GTCX supports risk reduction and transparency for development finance programs.

## Key Outcomes

- Verifiable impact evidence
- Reduced due‑diligence cost
- Better monitoring of funded projects

## Recommended Actions

- Require verification evidence in funding programs
- Integrate GTCX data into reporting
- Sponsor pilot verification corridors

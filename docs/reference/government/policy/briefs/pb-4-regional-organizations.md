# Policy Brief 4: Regional Organizations

## Purpose

Position GTCX as a regional trade and compliance standard to improve cross‑border interoperability.

## Key Outcomes

- Harmonized standards across member states
- Reduced regulatory duplication
- Shared evidence infrastructure

## Recommended Actions

- Publish regional verification guidelines
- Establish joint data governance protocols
- Coordinate pilot programs across borders

# GoldReg

## Purpose

Outline a regulatory framework for sovereign commodity verification that blends local oversight with global interoperability.

## Focus Areas

- Regulatory legitimacy for verified commodities
- Enforcement through evidence rather than paperwork
- Alignment with international trade standards

## Output

A public‑sector policy concept that can be adapted for specific jurisdictions.

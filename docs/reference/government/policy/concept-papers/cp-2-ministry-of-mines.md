# Concept Paper 2: Ministry of Mines (Extended)

## Concept

Expand the verification framework to include compliance training, workforce certification, and interoperability with trade systems.

## Components

- Training and accreditation pipeline
- Regulator reporting dashboards
- Interoperable verification APIs

## Pilot Scope

Multi‑region rollout with phased onboarding of producers and buyers.

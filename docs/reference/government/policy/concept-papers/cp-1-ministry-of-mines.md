# Concept Paper 1: Ministry of Mines

## Concept

A sovereign verification framework that shifts licensing and oversight toward evidence‑based compliance rather than paper‑driven audits.

## Components

- Verified producer registry
- Chain‑of‑custody verification
- Compliance evidence ledger

## Pilot Scope

One region, one commodity, and one export corridor to establish baseline results.

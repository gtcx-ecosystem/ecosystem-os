### MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

**Between:**

**[MINISTRY OF MINES AND MINERAL RESOURCES]** of [COUNTRY], hereinafter referred to as "the Ministry" or "Government"

**And:**

**GTCX FOUNDATION**, a Swiss foundation, hereinafter referred to as "GTCX"

**Collectively referred to as "the Parties"**

---

### RECITALS

**WHEREAS:**

A. The Government seeks to modernize mining sector governance through digital infrastructure that enhances revenue collection, supply chain transparency, and regulatory oversight while maintaining full sovereign control;

B. GTCX has developed open-source verification infrastructure (the "GTCX Protocol") designed for sovereign deployment and government ownership;

C. The Parties wish to collaborate on deploying GTCX infrastructure within [COUNTRY] under terms that ensure government sovereignty, data residency, and sustainable operations;

**NOW THEREFORE**, the Parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1** "GTCX Protocol" means the open-source software comprising TradePass™, GeoTag™, GCI™, VIA™, VXA™, and related components.

**1.2** "Sovereign Instance" means the deployment of GTCX Protocol on government-controlled infrastructure within [COUNTRY].

**1.3** "Implementation Services" means technical deployment, integration, training, and support services provided by GTCX.

**1.4** "Transaction Fee" means the per-transaction charge collected through the system for verified commodity movements.

**1.5** "Pilot Phase" means the initial limited deployment period as defined in Article 5.

---

### ARTICLE 2: SCOPE OF COLLABORATION

**2.1 Government Responsibilities:**

The Government shall:

a) Designate a lead ministry and task force for implementation coordination;

b) Provide or arrange infrastructure for hosting the Sovereign Instance;

c) Assign personnel for training and system administration;

d) Ensure regulatory framework supports digital verification recognition;

e) Collect and manage Transaction Fees through existing revenue mechanisms;

f) Promote adoption among mining operators and related stakeholders.

**2.2 GTCX Responsibilities:**

GTCX shall:

a) Provide the GTCX Protocol software under open-source license;

b) Deploy and configure the Sovereign Instance on Government infrastructure;

c) Integrate with designated government systems per agreed specifications;

d) Train government personnel on system administration and operations;

e) Provide ongoing technical support per agreed service levels;

f) Deliver software updates and security patches.

---

### ARTICLE 3: SOVEREIGNTY GUARANTEES

**3.1 Data Ownership:**

All data generated or processed by the Sovereign Instance is owned exclusively by the Government. GTCX shall have no claim to such data.

**3.2 Data Residency:**

All data shall be stored on servers physically located within [COUNTRY] or in locations approved in writing by the Government.

**3.3 Encryption Keys:**

Encryption keys for data protection shall be generated and held exclusively by the Government. GTCX shall not retain copies.

**3.4 Administrative Control:**

The Government shall have full administrative access to all system functions, including:

- User management and access control
- Policy rule configuration
- Data export and reporting
- System monitoring and auditing

**3.5 No External Dependencies:**

The Sovereign Instance shall operate independently of any GTCX-controlled infrastructure. System operation shall not require connectivity to GTCX servers.

**3.6 Source Code Access:**

The Government shall have full access to source code under the applicable open-source license, with right to modify, fork, or operate independently.

### ARTICLE 7: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**7.1 Pre-Existing IP:**

GTCX Protocol software remains subject to its open-source license (Apache 2.0 / MIT). Government receives perpetual, irrevocable license to use, modify, and distribute.

**7.2 Customizations:**

Customizations developed for the Government shall be owned by the Government with option to contribute to open-source project.

**7.3 Government Data:**

All data, reports, and analytics generated from Government data are owned exclusively by the Government.

---

### ARTICLE 8: CONFIDENTIALITY

**8.1 Confidential Information:**

Each Party shall protect the other's confidential information with reasonable care, but not less than the care used for its own confidential information.

**8.2 Exclusions:**

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed, or lawfully received from third parties.

**8.3 Government Transparency:**

Nothing in this Agreement prevents the Government from disclosing information as required by law or for public accountability purposes.

---

### ARTICLE 9: TERM AND TERMINATION

**9.1 Initial Term:**

This Agreement shall be effective from the date of signing and continue for [5] years unless terminated earlier.

**9.2 Renewal:**

The Agreement may be renewed for additional [3]-year terms by mutual written agreement.

**9.3 Termination for Convenience:**

Either Party may terminate with [180] days written notice.

**9.4 Termination for Cause:**

Either Party may terminate immediately upon material breach that remains uncured for [60] days after written notice.

**9.5 Effect of Termination:**

Upon termination:

a) Government retains all data and may continue operating the software under open-source license;

b) GTCX shall provide [90] days transition support;

c) GTCX shall deliver all documentation and training materials;

d) Outstanding fees shall be settled within [30] days.

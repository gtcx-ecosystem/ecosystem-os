# GTCX 4-Layer Model

GTCX is composed of five interconnected layers, each responsible for enforcing trust, compliance, and verifiable execution across the physical commodity trade lifecycle:

1. **L0 – Data & Field Infrastructure**\
   Capture origin, custody, certification, and actor data directly in the field using tools like GeoTag™, CustodyCheck™, and mobile onboarding kits.
2. **L1 – Compliance Logic & Scoring** _(GCI, ComReg)_\
   Convert field data into actionable trust signals using standardized rulesets and real-time compliance scoring.
3. **L2 – Credentialed Identity & Access** _(TradePass™)_\
   Grant platform permissions based on verified roles, certification status, and risk scores.
4. **L3 – Secure Execution & Discharge** _(AGX)_\
   Coordinate verified trades, enforce escrow and settlement logic, and authorize compliant vault or customs release.
5. **L4 – Sovereign Observability & Enforcement** _(SGX)_\
   Enable regulators and sovereign actors to monitor, approve, or block transactions with full traceability and legal authority.

# Page 1

#### An Accessible Modular, Scalable Framework&#x20;

The GTCX ecosystem operates through distinct but integrated layers that provide clarity for stakeholder engagement while enabling systematic scaling across geographic markets and commodity sectors.&#x20;

The architecture operates through a **3-Tier Operational Model**:

<table><thead><tr><th width="147.36328125" align="center">Layer</th><th width="593.1328125">High-level Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td align="center"><p>T<strong>1</strong></p><p><code>Protocol</code></p></td><td><strong>Universal Standards</strong><br>Establishes universal technical rules, compliance protocols, and API standards. This layer ensures interoperability across jurisdictions while maintaining open, verifiable governance to prevent centralization or capture.</td></tr><tr><td align="center"><p><strong>T2</strong></p><p><code>Operations</code></p></td><td><strong>Field Infrastructure + Ops</strong><br>Translates protocol standards into real-world service delivery through tools like digital identity, GeoTag, and GCI. This field-first layer enables onboarding, compliance scoring, and verification for producers with varying technological access—connecting informal supply chains to formal global markets.</td></tr><tr><td align="center"><p><strong>T3</strong></p><p><code>Exchange</code></p></td><td><strong>Trade Exchange</strong><br>Powers localized yet authenticated trade environments where verified producers access pricing, liquidity, and market discovery through dashboards, trade terminals, and settlement systems. This layer preserves sovereign governance while enabling global exchange integration.</td></tr></tbody></table>

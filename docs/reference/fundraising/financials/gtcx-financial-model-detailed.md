| **Minimum Viable**   | $2.8M     | $2.4M  | $4.2M    | Works if everything goes right |

| **Aggressive Scale** | $6.0M     | $5.0M  | $9.0M    | If expanding to 2nd country    |

## Key Assumptions & Risks

### Assumptions Built Into These Numbers

1. **Hiring goes smoothly** — CTO and leads hired within 2 months
2. **Ghana MOU signed** — Government partnership confirmed by Month 4
3. **No major pivots** — Core architecture remains stable
4. **Biometric SDK works** — No major integration issues
5. **Field conditions manageable** — Devices and connectivity adequate
6. **Exchange rate stable** — USD/GHS doesn't swing dramatically

### What Could Go Wrong (and Contingency)

| Risk                                 | Impact          | Mitigation             | Contingency |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------- | ---------------------- | ----------- |
| CTO hire takes 6 months              | 3-month delay   | Start with senior IC   | +$150K      |
| Ghana MOU delayed                    | 4-month delay   | Parallel Rwanda track  | +$200K      |
| Biometric SDK integration hard       | 2-month delay   | Budget for custom work | +$75K       |
| Security audit fails                 | 2-month delay   | Early internal review  | +$100K      |
| Field conditions worse than expected | Cost overruns   | Local partnerships     | +$100K      |
| Exchange rate moves 20%              | Budget pressure | USD-denominated costs  | +$150K      |

**Total risk buffer in "Comfortable" scenario: ~$350K (10%)**

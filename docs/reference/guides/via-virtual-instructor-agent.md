### 1 – What is VIA?

**Your AI Compliance Teacher and Readiness Coach**

VIA (Virtual Instructor Agent) is an AI-powered training and guidance system that teaches compliance requirements and helps organizations prepare for verification—without requiring expensive consultants or travel to training centers.

Available 24/7 via mobile app, WhatsApp, SMS, and web browser, VIA delivers:

- **Adaptive micro-lessons** tailored to your role, sector, and experience level
- **Interactive readiness assessments** showing exactly what evidence you need
- **Multilingual instruction** in 20+ languages including African languages
- **Offline-capable** learning that works anywhere, anytime

**Think of VIA as the compliance teacher that meets you where you are—whether you're an artisanal miner in rural Zambia or a compliance officer in Lagos.**

### 5 – How does VIA work?

#### Adaptive Learning Engine

VIA personalizes instruction based on:

**Your Context:**

- **Sector**: Mining, agriculture, manufacturing, forestry, fisheries
- **Scale**: Artisanal, small-scale, medium, large industrial
- **Geography**: Country, region, language, local regulations
- **Role**: Owner, manager, worker, inspector, community member

**Your Current State:**

- **Experience Level**: New to compliance, basic understanding, intermediate, expert
- **Knowledge Gaps**: Initial assessment identifies what you need to learn
- **Evidence Readiness**: What documentation you already have vs. what you need
- **Learning Style**: Visual, text-based, audio, interactive scenarios

#### Learning Pathways

**Quick Start (30 minutes)**

- What is compliance and why it matters
- The 12 domains overview
- What evidence looks like
- Next steps for your organization

**Sector Foundations (2-4 hours)**

- Sector-specific requirements (mining, agriculture, etc.)
- Common practices in your operational context
- Evidence examples from similar organizations
- Readiness self-assessment

**Control Deep Dives (20-40 minutes each)**

- Detailed instruction on specific controls
- What auditors will look for
- How to document compliance
- Common mistakes and how to avoid them

**Verification Preparation (1-2 hours)**

- Mock audit scenarios
- Evidence review and gap identification
- Documentation organization
- Auditor interaction guidance

#### Micro-Learning Format

**5-10 Minute Lessons:**

- Short video demonstrations
- Interactive scenarios and simulations
- Knowledge checks with immediate feedback
- Practical exercises (document your own practice)
- Progress tracking and badges

**Works Offline:**

- Download lessons when connected
- Complete training anywhere
- Sync progress when back online
- SMS-based interaction for ultra-low bandwidth

#### Multilingual & Culturally Adapted

**Language Support:**

- English, French, Portuguese, Spanish
- Swahili, Hausa, Amharic, Yoruba, Zulu
- Local dialects and indigenous languages
- Audio instruction for low-literacy contexts

**Cultural Adaptation:**

- Examples from your region and sector
- Respectful of traditional practices and governance
- Gender-sensitive content and delivery
- Integration of indigenous knowledge with formal standards

### 6 – Who uses VIA?

#### Primary User Journeys:

**Artisanal Mining Cooperative (Ghana)**

_Sarah manages a 45-member artisanal gold mining cooperative. They want to access formal markets but don't understand certification requirements._

**VIA Journey:**

- Signs up via mobile app (no computer needed)
- Takes 10-minute assessment identifies as "new to compliance, mining sector, Ghana"
- Receives custom learning path: "Artisanal Mining Compliance Essentials"
- Completes lessons in Twi language during evening hours
- Gets readiness report: "You're 40% prepared. Focus on these 8 controls next."
- Downloads evidence checklist and documentation templates
- After 3 weeks: Ready for GCI verification with documented practices

**Agricultural Cooperative (Zambia)**

_James runs a coffee cooperative with 200 smallholder farmers. They need Fairtrade certification but can't afford consultant._

**VIA Journey:**

- Accesses VIA through cooperative's shared tablet
- Selects "Agriculture Coffee Cooperative Manager Zambia"
- Learns about required farmer records, quality controls, environmental practices
- Uses VIA's evidence templates to organize existing documentation
- VIA identifies gaps: "Need written environmental policy, grievance mechanism documentation"
- Gets step-by-step guidance on creating missing documents
- After 6 weeks: Documents organized, cooperative members trained, ready for certification audit

**Government Mining Inspector (Tanzania)**

_David needs to conduct inspections across 50 small-scale mining sites but lacks standardized training._

**VIA Journey:**

- Government provides VIA access for all inspectors
- Completes "OCP Verification Methodology for Mining Sector" (40 hours)
- Learns Core12 controls and evidence assessment protocols
- Practices with mock inspection scenarios
- Gets digital certification as OCP-qualified inspector
- Uses VIA's mobile inspection checklists during site visits
- Generates standardized reports recognized by ministry and international buyers

**Institutional knowledge accessible to everyone—democratizing compliance education and enabling universal participation in formal markets.**

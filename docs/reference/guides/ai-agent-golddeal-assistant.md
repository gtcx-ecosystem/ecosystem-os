# AI Agent: "GoldDeal Assistant"

> **Status:** Current
> **Date:** 2026-05-22
> **Owner:** Protocol Architect

#### Core Value Proposition

Transform a 3-6 week manual process into a 3-5 day AI-orchestrated transaction, reducing costs by 60% while improving compliance accuracy and documentation quality.

#### Multi-Agent Architecture

**1. KYC/AML Compliance Agent**

**What it handles:**

- Automated supplier background checks across 25+ global databases
- Real-time sanctions screening (OFAC, EU, UN consolidated lists)
- Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) verification
- PEP (Politically Exposed Person) screening
- Anti-money laundering risk scoring
- Generates compliant KYC packages for internal compliance teams

**Current manual process:** 5-7 days **AI-automated:** 2-4 hours

**Example interaction:**

> "I've completed KYC verification for Ashanti Gold Cooperative. Clean results across all databases. UBO identified as John Mensah (Ghana national, no PEP/sanctions flags). Risk score: Low. Full report ready for your compliance team."

**2. Supply Chain Compliance (SCO) Agent**

**What it handles:**

- OECD Due Diligence verification for responsible gold sourcing
- Conflict-free minerals certification
- Environmental compliance documentation
- Social impact assessment (working conditions, community impact)
- Chain of custody verification
- Integration with GCTX verification systems where available

**Current manual process:** 2-3 weeks **AI-enhanced:** 1-2 days

**Example output:**

> "Supply chain analysis complete for proposed 50kg purchase. Source: Tarkwa mining region (non-conflict zone). Environmental compliance: 92/100 score. No child labor flags. Mercury-free processing verified via GCTX network. Ready to proceed."

**3. Banking & Settlement Coordination Agent**

**What it handles:**

- Secure exchange of banking details via encrypted channels
- SWIFT message preparation and coordination
- Payment scheduling and confirmation tracking
- Multi-currency considerations and FX hedging options
- Escrow account coordination when required
- Payment-versus-delivery (PvD) optimization

**Traditional pain point eliminated:** No more email chains with banking details, manual SWIFT coordination, or payment timing mismatches.

**Example coordination:**

> "Banking details exchanged securely. Supplier's account verified with Ecobank Ghana (SWIFT: ECOCGHAC). Suggested payment structure: 30% on contract signature, 70% on delivery confirmation. Escrow service recommended for first transaction."

**4. Legal Documentation Agent**

**What it handles:**

- Contract generation using proven templates adapted for specific terms
- Jurisdiction-specific legal requirement compliance
- Risk allocation and liability optimization
- Force majeure and delivery term customization
- Integration with existing legal review processes
- Amendment and revision management

**Reduces legal fees from $15-25k to $3-5k per transaction**

**Example service:**

> "Purchase agreement drafted: 50kg at LBMA PM +2.8% premium. Delivery terms: CIF Antwerp. Payment: Letter of credit. Ghana law governs, London arbitration. Ready for your legal team's final review."

**5. Cultural Intelligence & Negotiation Agent**

**What it handles:**

- Cultural context for West African business practices
- Communication style adaptation and translation
- Relationship-building strategies
- Gift and hospitality guidance (compliance-appropriate)
- Timing and protocol recommendations
- Conflict resolution and mediation support

**Eliminates cultural misunderstandings that delay deals by weeks**

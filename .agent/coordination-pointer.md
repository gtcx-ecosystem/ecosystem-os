# Coordination Pointer

Durable cross-repo coordination for `ecosystem-os` is recorded under
`ops/coordination/` for operational handoffs and `docs/operations/coordination/`
for human-readable repo documentation.

Fleet-level QASC, AaaS, and assurance evidence is provisioned by `fabric-os`.
Protocol and layout authority is provisioned by `canon-os` and `bridge-os`.
